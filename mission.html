About Mission Road Map

Our mission is to allow everyone to invest in real estate

To break us free from a rigged financial system. We’re building generational wealth through numbers. Chances are, if your forefathers didn’t snatch up all the cheap land 100’s of years ago for you to inherit, you’re at a disadvantage. You’re also at a disadvantage if no one in your family was around to pick up stocks early in Wall Street during the Industrial boom, cashed out in the midst of the dotcom bubble, or even more recently with all the tech stocks (like buying amazon back when it was $25 per share). Sure, getting in early is key to cashing out a major return but who has a crystal ball to know where, when, and what to invest in? We know one thing is certain, Real estate has always recovered and weathered every economic crash. It’s not only a safe play but an exponential one. Traditional banks take your hard earned money, give you close to nothing in interest, all while turning around and lending it out to people just like you and me to make “bank” (no pun intended). The bank’s interest doesn’t even match inflation. We need a new currency, backed by real assets, growing through value add and rental cash flow. That’s a serious domino effect when your coin’s value keeps growing and making money on its own. Welcome to a new bank (REI the people), a new currency (REI Coin), backed by real cash flowing assets.
