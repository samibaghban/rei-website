How To Buy REI Coin?

About REI Coin

REI is simple. We have issued 10B REI coins and every quarter we sell 10% to raise Money to buy distressed real estate. We rehab the properties and rent them out. This does two things for coin holders: 1) Your coin is backed by real estate that will go up in value through “sweat equity” (rehabbing). 2) Your coin’s actually making money through monthly rent collection. You’ll always know exactly what the coin is worth fundamentally by taking total coins divided by the total value of properties plus cash flow. You’ll also be able to earn interest by holding REI coins because as a community you’ve become the defi bank that powers this whole eco system. The community will vote on which properties to buy next and every round will introduce new coin holders at a higher price. Don’t worry, adding backers every quarter will only allow us to buy more properties as a community and exponentially grow our cash flow potential. We will eventually get to a point that our monthly cash flow will allow us to purchase property every month and require tenants to pay in REI coins. This is how generational wealth is built, but we’ve hacked the system by joining forces as a community.

REI Coin is powered by real estate

It’s a virtual coin with physical assets. Real Estate is at our core (residential, multi family, and commercial), we want the most diversified portfolio but you ultimately get to decide the route we take. We’ll publish our property finds as well as community submitted finds for us all to vote on. All of the prospective properties need to pass the following criteria.

  • Subject property is selling for 30% or more below market value.
  • Comparable rentals within the area have occupancies within 80-90%
  • All-In rehab costs will still provide us with a profit margin of 20% or more
