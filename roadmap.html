Road Map

The REICoin team has outlined an aggressive but achievable timeline below. Each quarter has its own milestones and deliverables we are activily working towards.

Q1 2021

We assembled our core advisory team of real estate professionals and tech gurus, Integrated UNITvpm to rehab, maintain and manage properties within the REI portfolio, and have identified a list of properties to secure.

Q2 2021

The time has come We will have our Initial Coin Offering. Proceeds will be used to acquire and rehab distressed properties. Turning them all cash-flow positive within 4 months. We will integrate REI coin into the UNITvpm app as the only form of payment.

Q3 2021

Appraise and raise! We will Apprise the community’s properties to reflect the added equity built. We will build a dashboard for the community to asses, vote, and provide valuable feedback on the direction we take with the cash flow (buy additional property or buy back coins). This process creates a dynamic cycle of adding value to our coin through buying properties on a discount due to their current condition and rehabbing them (earned “sweat” equity). Then we rent the rehabbed properties to create residual income for REI (residual/passive value). We are aiming to make REI the greatest hyper growth coin in the market and your support and input will help get us there!

Q4 2021

We will have our first blockchain protocol completed. This will include vital building information (think of it as a Carfax for commercial properties). The data will allow the community to monitor and track financials, building health, and compare various strategies (hold and operate, sell, or sub-lease).

Q1 2022

Rinse and Repeat Every Quarter from here on out, REI will offer 10% of its held supply of 10B coins. The process will allow us to take advantage of the increased price and acquire more and more real estate to back our collective REI coins. It’s pretty simple, The longer you hold...the more properties you’ll have ownership in. We will be working hard to continuously build new features that will help us use our blockchain protocol to monetize in other aspects of real estate (smart contracts, title search, escrow, smart tenant screening, and county recordings to list a few of the features we are planing to bring into the market).
